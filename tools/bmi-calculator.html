BMI Calculator with Age and Gender

This advanced BMI calculator allows you to calculate your body mass index based on weight, height, age, and gender.

How BMI is Calculated?

BMI is calculated using weight divided by height squared. Age and gender help provide better health insights.

BMI Categories

Underweight: Below 18.5 | Normal: 18.5–24.9 | Overweight: 25–29.9 | Obese: 30+
