What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation that uses your weight and height to estimate body fat. It is one of the most common methods used to assess whether your body weight is healthy or not.

How BMI is Calculated?

BMI is calculated using the formula: weight (kg) divided by height (m²). This calculator also considers age and gender to give better health insights and recommendations.

How to Use BMI Calculator?

Enter your weight in kilograms, height in meters or centimeters, and provide your age and gender. Click on calculate to instantly get your BMI value and health category.

BMI Categories

✔ Underweight: Below 18.5
✔ Normal weight: 18.5 – 24.9
✔ Overweight: 25 – 29.9
✔ Obese: 30 and above

FAQs - BMI Calculator

1. Is BMI accurate?
BMI gives a general idea but may not be 100% accurate for athletes or bodybuilders.

2. Why include age and gender?
Age and gender help provide more accurate health insights and interpretation.

3. What is a healthy BMI?
A BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered healthy.
