What is Fixed Deposit (FD)?

A Fixed Deposit (FD) is a popular and safe investment option where you deposit a fixed amount of money for a specific period and earn guaranteed interest on it. FD is widely used in India by individuals who want stable returns with low risk. Banks and financial institutions offer different interest rates based on tenure and investment amount.

What is FD Calculator?

An FD calculator is an online tool that helps you calculate the maturity amount and interest earned on your fixed deposit. It uses a compound interest formula to give accurate results instantly without manual calculations.

How FD Calculator Works?

The FD calculator works on the compound interest formula, where interest is calculated on both the principal amount and accumulated interest. By entering your deposit amount, interest rate, and tenure, you can instantly see your maturity value.

How to Use FD Calculator?

Enter your investment amount, select the interest rate, and choose the time period. Click on calculate to get your maturity amount and total interest earned instantly.
