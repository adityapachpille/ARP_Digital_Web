What is EMI (Equated Monthly Installment)?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount you pay every month to repay a loan. It includes both the principal amount and the interest charged by the bank or financial institution. EMI is commonly used for home loans, car loans, personal loans, and education loans in India.

What is EMI Calculator?

An EMI calculator is an online tool that helps you calculate your monthly loan payments easily. By entering loan amount, interest rate, and tenure, you can instantly know your EMI amount without manual calculation.

How to Calculate EMI?

EMI is calculated using a standard formula based on loan amount, interest rate, and loan tenure. This calculator automatically calculates EMI along with total interest payable and total payment.
