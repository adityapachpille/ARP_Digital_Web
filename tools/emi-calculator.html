EMI Calculator

What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount you pay every month towards your loan. It includes both principal and interest components.

How to Calculate EMI?

EMI is calculated using a standard formula based on loan amount, interest rate, and tenure. Our EMI calculator helps you calculate EMI instantly without manual calculation.

Benefits of EMI Calculator

This EMI calculator is useful for planning your loans such as home loan, car loan, or personal loan. It helps you understand your monthly payment and manage your finances better.
