What is SIP Calculator?

A SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) calculator helps you estimate the future value of your monthly investments in mutual funds. It is widely used in India to plan long-term wealth creation.

How SIP Calculator Works?

The SIP calculator uses compound interest formula to calculate your returns based on monthly investment, expected return rate, and time period. It gives you an estimate of your total investment and wealth gained.

Benefits of SIP Investment

SIP helps you invest small amounts regularly, reduce risk, and build wealth over time. It is one of the best investment options for long-term financial goals.
