What is Percentage?

Percentage is a number or ratio expressed as a fraction of 100. It is widely used in mathematics, business, finance, and daily life calculations. For example, 50% means 50 out of 100. Percentages are commonly used to calculate discounts, marks, profit and loss, interest rates, and many other values.

What is Percentage Calculator?

How to Calculate Percentage?

Percentage can be calculated using the formula: (Value / Total Value) × 100. You can also calculate percentage of a number by multiplying the number by the percentage and dividing by 100. This calculator performs all these calculations instantly.
