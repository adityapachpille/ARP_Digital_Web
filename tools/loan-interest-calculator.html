Loan Interest Calculator

What is Loan Interest?

Loan interest is the cost you pay to a bank or lender for borrowing money. It is usually expressed as a percentage of the principal amount and is paid along with the loan amount. Interest can be simple or compound depending on the type of loan.

What is Interest Calculator?

An interest calculator is an online tool that helps you calculate the total interest payable on a loan. It quickly calculates interest and total amount based on principal, rate, and time without manual calculations.

How to Calculate Interest?

Interest is calculated using the formula: Principal × Rate × Time / 100. This calculator uses the same formula to give you accurate results instantly. You just need to enter the loan amount, interest rate, and time period.

How to Use Loan Interest Calculator?

Enter the principal amount, interest rate, and time period. Click on calculate to get the total interest and final amount payable. This helps you plan your loan repayment easily.
