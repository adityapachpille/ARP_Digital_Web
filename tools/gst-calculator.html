What is GST (Goods and Services Tax)?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is an indirect tax applied to the supply of goods and services in India. It was introduced to simplify the complex tax system by replacing multiple indirect taxes like VAT, service tax, and excise duty. GST is charged at different rates such as 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28% depending on the product or service.

What is GST Calculator?

A GST calculator is an online tool that helps you calculate GST amount quickly and accurately. It allows you to add GST to the original price or remove GST from the total amount instantly. This tool is useful for business owners, shopkeepers, freelancers, and individuals.

How to Calculate GST?

GST is calculated by applying a percentage on the original price of goods or services. For example, if the GST rate is 18%, you need to multiply the price by 18% to get the GST amount. This calculator performs the calculation instantly and shows both GST amount and final price.

How to Use GST Calculator?
